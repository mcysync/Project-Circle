LineageOS based Lightweight Fine Crafted OS

Features

Devshell Derivation

[For developers] Devshell derivation for building the ROM on NixOS.

Lightweight GApps

Small GApps package perfectly optimized for GApps builds.

Clean Face Unlock

Face unlock without the ugly face icon on the lock screen.

Google Feed

Seamlessly integrated Google Feed on the Trebuchet launcher.

Advanced Spoofing

Developer options, PIF, Tricky Store, and per-app individual device spoofing.

Flashlight Levels

Granular flashlight level adjustment to suit your environment.

Pocket Lock

Smart pocket lock detection prevents accidental touches.

AOD Wallpaper

Wallpaper visible on Always-On Display (unless force disabled by maintainer).

Lineage Foundation

All core LineageOS features preserved and optimized.
