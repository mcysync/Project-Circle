Project CiRCLE!

This project is an aftermarket Android firmware based on LineageOS, with hand-picked features from various ROMs.

Including, but not limited to, Play Integrity spoofing with custom pif.json and keybox, per-app device spoofing for apps that refuse to work in certain conditions like Samsung AOSP with Samsung apps, and wallpaper in AOD.

A fresh design style that complements both the "BanG Dream! Girls Band Party" mobile game and Google's Material Design 3 Expressive update.
